Supplementary File 2. Stage-model development and freezing details
Study: Retrospective validation of a safety-governed multi-agent pathway controller for suspected acute aortic syndrome

This file preserves the detailed feature-view, preprocessing, threshold-selection, and frozen-artefact crosswalk that was moved out of the Supplementary Information PDF during compression. All stage models were trained or selected using Cohort D only. The resulting artefacts were shared unchanged by the canonical threshold pathway, single-agent baseline, and multi-agent controller.

Stage-model development and freezing details for the shared quantitative risk substrate
