Supplementary Data 4 manifest

File: supplementary_data_4_cohort_v2_negative_strata_metrics.csv
Description: AD-negative reference-standard evidence-stratum action metrics in Cohort V2.
Row scope: Cohort V2 adjudicated AD-negative patients only.
Strata:
- overall_final_ad_negative: all 11,042 adjudicated AD-negative patients.
- cta_mra_retained_negative: AD-negative records classified as CTA/MRA-retained negative in the post hoc evidence-stratum analysis.
- non_cta_mra_retained_negative: AD-negative records not classified as CTA/MRA-retained negative in the post hoc evidence-stratum analysis.
Methods:
- ADD-RS >=1
- Canonical threshold
- Single-agent
- Multi-agent
Metric definitions:
- n_negatives: number of adjudicated AD-negative patients in the stratum.
- ad_negative_assigned_escalation: AD-negative patients assigned terminal direct CTA pathway or urgent specialist/transfer pathway escalation.
- ad_negative_clinician_overseen_reassessment: AD-negative patients assigned clinician-overseen reassessment; this is the action-derived specificity numerator.
- specificity_within_negative_stratum: ad_negative_clinician_overseen_reassessment / n_negatives within the stratum.
- ad_negative_assigned_escalation_rate: ad_negative_assigned_escalation / n_negatives within the stratum.
- direct_cta_pathway: terminal direct CTA pathway assignments within the stratum when available for the method.
- urgent_specialist_transfer_pathway_escalation: terminal urgent specialist/transfer pathway escalation assignments within the stratum when available for the method.
- clinician_overseen_reassessment: terminal clinician-overseen reassessment assignments within the stratum.
Interpretation boundary: CTA/MRA-retained negative and non-CTA/MRA-retained negative are reference-standard evidence strata within the pragmatic clinically adjudicated AD-negative denominator. They do not define uniform imaging-confirmed diagnostic specificity and did not alter labels, model inputs, thresholds, prompts, deterministic safety-governance rules, or terminal actions.
